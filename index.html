WORKING PAPER: Financial Inclusion in Rural Peru and Agricultural Burnings

Within the context of changing financial landscape of the peruvian economy, the effects of policy pushing for greater financial inclusion may be a mechanism that has allowed for a shift in agricultural burnings- and the effects of financial inclusion on villages and communities where agricultural burnings may have previously been a necessity may result in a shift in agricultural practices, which we predict may include a decreased reliance on fire.
